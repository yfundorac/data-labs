Featured content in Wikipedia

Featured content represents the best of Wikipedia, including articles, pictures, and other contributions that showcase excellent results of the collaborative efforts of Wikipedia. All featured content undergoes a thorough review process to ensure that it meets the highest standards in order to serve as the best example of our end goals.

A small bronze star () in the top right corner of a desktop page indicates that the content is featured.

This page gives links to all of Wikipedia's featured content.
